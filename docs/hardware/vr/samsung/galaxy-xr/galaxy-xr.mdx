# Galaxy XR

The Galaxy XR, running on AndroidXR, provides eye gaze tracking, detailed eye expression tracking, and face tracking.
All eye and face tracking hardware is internal to the device (no additional hardware setup required).
All eye and face tracking data is accessed via AndroidXR Face and Eye OpenXR extensions.

## Setup

The basic Galaxy XR setup flow:
1. [Calibrate Eye Tracking on the Headset](#eye-tracking-calibration)
2. [Calibrate Face Tracking on the Headset](#face-tracking-calibration)
3. [PCVR Setup](#pcvr-setup)

### Eye Tracking Calibration
To calibrate eye tracking, open the Settings app, select "Input", "Eye calibration", then "Calibrate eye tracking".
Follow the on-screen instructions.

### Face Tracking Calibration
:::danger
It is vital to initially calibrate face tracking for both Face & Eye tracking to fully work.
:::
To calibrate face tracking, open the Settings app, then select "Avatars", then select "Start Calibration" under "Calibrate Face Tracking" and follow the on-screen instructions.
